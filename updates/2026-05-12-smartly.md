## Acquisition of INCRMNTAL Completed
urgency: urgent
date: May 8, 2026
source: Signalbase M&A News · May 8, 2026
url: https://www.trysignalbase.com/news/acquisitions/incrmntal-acquired-by-smartly-acquisition

Smartly completed its acquisition of INCRMNTAL, an AI incrementality measurement platform, closing the deal announced via Letter of Intent on March 16, 2026. INCRMNTAL (founded 2020 by Maor Sadra and Moti Tal) uses causal AI to measure advertising effectiveness without relying on user-level data — making it a privacy-centric measurement solution. The acquisition directly strengthens Smartly's full-funnel offering: it can now close the loop between creative execution and true business incrementality in a single platform. For ROI Hunter: Smartly is rapidly becoming a more complete platform (creative → execution → measurement), which makes the competitive comparison harder to win on breadth alone. ROI Hunter should emphasize depth of catalog/DPA-specific capabilities and ease of use for ecommerce teams vs. Smartly's enterprise complexity.
